ABOUT

Back in 2022, I decided to try my hand at coding through Matt Delac's course, SheCodes Max and have not looked back since. Fastforward to today, I have picked up HTML, CSS, Javascript and React, and am looking forward to put these skills to use.

After 8 years in marketing, I'm transitioning to front-end development to merge my creative experience with hands-on technical proficiency.

My main focus these days is building projects on Github to hone my skills. When I’m not at the computer, I’m usually swinging weights at Crossfit, watching TV with my husband and cat, or checking out restaurants on the coast.
